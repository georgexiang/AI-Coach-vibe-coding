## Solution Overview

The AI Coach platform enables Medical Representatives (MRs) to practice and improve their HCP engagement skills through AI-powered simulations:

- **F2F HCP Engagement**: One-on-one role-play with virtual doctors
- **Conference Presentation**: One-to-many virtual department presentations
- **Multi-dimensional Scoring**: Real-time feedback and post-session analysis
- **Training Management**: Centralized content, progress tracking, and reporting
